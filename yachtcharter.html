About Us

Since 2013, our company has been offering yacht services in the Ionian Sea, specializing in yacht and catamaran charters, yacht management, and comprehensive yacht services.

We started with a focus on delivering exceptional charter experiences and have grown year by year, expanding our fleet and offerings to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our yacht management services handle all aspects of private yacht investment, from organization to administration, while our yacht services include certifications, legal support, permits, supplies, and maintenance.

As we continue to grow, we prioritize quality, reliability, and strong relationships with partners.
