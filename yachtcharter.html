Types of charter we provide

Bareboat Yacht Charters

A bareboat charter allows you to rent an entire yacht, requiring a valid sailing license and experience. With no crew, provisions, or third-party assistance included, this option provides full privacy and the flexibility to plan and adjust your itinerary each day.

Skippered Yacht Charters

A skippered charter is a bareboat charter with a professional skipper on board. This option is ideal for those without the required experience to navigate Greek waters or those seeking a stress-free vacation. Our local skippers not only handle the sailing but also share valuable insights about your destinations and Ionic Sea as a whole.
