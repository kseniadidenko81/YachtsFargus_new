Cyclades 43.3 "Sole Mio"

The Beneteau Cyclades 43.3 - Sole Mio is a 3-cabin sailing yacht built in 2005 and renovated in 2021.

Designed for comfort, Sole Mio accommodates up to 8 guests across 3 double cabins and a saloon with 2 extra beds. The yacht features 3 toilets with showers for added convenience.

Equipped for smooth sailing, it includes a classic mainsail, radar, salon GPS plotter, bimini, and bow thruster. Onboard entertainment and comfort are enhanced with a flat-screen TV, heating, audio system, DVD player, and outdoor speakers.

The fully equipped galley comes with a fridge, oven, cooker, sink, and kitchen utensils, ensuring a hassle-free culinary experience at sea.
